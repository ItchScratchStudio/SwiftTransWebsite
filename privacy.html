Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

At TransPop, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our macOS application.

Data Collection and Usage

TransPop is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect personal information directly. Your translation history and settings are stored locally on your device.

Translation Services

To provide translation functionality, text you submit is sent to the translation provider you select:

  • Google Translate: Text is sent to Google's translation API. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Bing Translator: Text is sent to Microsoft's Bing Translator service. Please refer to Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • OpenAI / Custom APIs: If you configure OpenAI or other API endpoints, data is sent to those services according to your configuration. Please refer to the privacy policy of the respective API provider (e.g., OpenAI Privacy Policy).

TransPop does not store your submitted text on any external servers other than the necessary transmission to these third-party providers for the purpose of translation.

Local Storage

  • Translation History: Stored locally on your Mac. You can clear this history at any time within the app settings.
  • API Keys: If you provide your own API keys (e.g., for OpenAI), they are stored securely in your macOS Keychain.
  • Preferences: App settings (theme, language, etc.) are stored in standard macOS User Defaults.

Crash Reporting

We may collect anonymous crash logs to help us improve the application stability. These logs do not contain personal user data or translation content.
